Front-end Developer Needed

Vivasoft, a leading software outsourcing company, is seeking a talented Senior Vue.js Developer to join our dynamic team. As a Senior Vue.js Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing software solutions using the Vue.js. You will work closely with our clients to understand their needs and translate those needs into effective software solutions. Additionally, you will mentor and guide junior developers on the team, ensuring that all code is of the highest quality.

Responsibilities:

  • We are seeking dedicated Front-end developers who are motivated to combine the art of design with the art of programming for our growing company.
  • The candidates will be able to use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React to translate business logic into a functioning frontend design.
  • You will be responsible for building fluid and responsive user interfaces, profiling and improving front-end performance, documenting our front-end codebase, writing tests for new features as well as maintaining existing projects.
  • Write high-quality, efficient, and maintainable JavaScript code.
  • Additionally, candidates will also be able to interact effectively with the rest of the development team and manage a group of young front-end developers.

Requirements:

  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript, including understanding of the object model, DOM manipulation and event handlers, data structures, algorithms, and Babel.
  • Complete knowledge of Vue.js and its core concepts, including the Virtual DOM, and component lifecycle.
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • Understanding of RESTful APIs/GraphQL, HTML/CSS, ES6 (variables and scoping, array methods), popular front-end development tools like Mocha, Node + NPM, code versioning tools like Git.
  • 4-6 years experience in frontend development.
  • Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git).
  • Familiarity with code review processes and mentoring junior developers.
  • Strong knowledge of TypeScript, Nuxt.js, and Vuex.
  • Familiarity with version control systems.
  • Up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies in web development.
  • Good problem-solving and troubleshooting skills.
  • Interest in playing (football, cricket) and singing is definitely a plus.

What we offer:

  • Good working environment.
  • Weekly holiday: 2 Days
  • Festival bonus: 2
  • Friendly work environment (Game room, gym facilities, music corner)
  • Scope for professional development & career growth
  • If you want to be prepared for the FAANG interview, we will assign a mentor for you.
  • Other perks as per company policy
